Chipotle Chicken Recipe

Ingredients Recipes

  • 1 Tsp Cumin Powder
  • 4 Garlic Cloves - Minced
  • 1 Tsp Dried Oregano
  • 2 Tsp Kosher Salt
  • 1 Tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon Chili Powder
  • 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil or Sunflower Oil
  • 7 Oz Can of Chipotle Peppers in Adobo Sauce (only use 4 peppers from the can)
  • 3/4 Cup of Water
  • 4-6 Boneless, Skinless Chicken Thighs

Direction

  1. Combine all of the spices and and ingrendients (except for the chicken) on low setting in a blender for 1 or 2 minutes
  2. Put the marinade in a ziplock bag along with the chicken and let it rest in the fridgerator for at least 8 hours
  3. After marinading the chickens, grill the chicken on medium to high heat for around 5-10 minutes until the grill marks are visibile
  4. Make sure the temperature inside the chicken are at least 165 Fareheit degrees
  5. Cut up the chicken however you want
  6. Enjoy
