Protein Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • ½ cup ice
  • 1 cup mixed frozen berries
  • 1 banana, peeled
  • 1 cup milk or milk alternative
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • ¼ cup whole oats

Direction

  1. Place all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until singular consistency.
  3. Pour in a glass and serve
